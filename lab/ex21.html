Department of Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering provides a strong foundation in computer systems, programming, and software engineering. Our mission is to educate students in the fundamentals of computing to prepare them for careers in software development, research, and higher studies.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Web and Mobile Application Development
  • Computer Networks and Cyber Security

Facilities:

  • Well-equipped Computer Labs
  • 24/7 Internet Access
  • Project Incubation Center
  • Research and Innovation Cell
